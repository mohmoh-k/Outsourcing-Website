What is Sayarma

Mobile phones only became ubiquitous in Myanmar after 2011, so unsurprisingly, general levels of Digital Literacy are low. This digital literacy app teaches users the basics of using a mobile phone, tablet and laptop so they can get the most out of them. It also covers the use of apps and software as well as Media Literacy, so that users learn how to safely and responsibly participate in platforms such as Facebook.
